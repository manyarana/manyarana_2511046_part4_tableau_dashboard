# Business Insights

## Insight 1: Sales Remain Stable Throughout the Year

### Observation
Monthly sales remain relatively stable throughout the year with only moderate fluctuations.

### Data Evidence
The Monthly Sales Trend chart shows monthly sales ranging approximately between **15M and 20M**, with the highest sales occurring around February and October.

### Business Interpretation
The business maintains consistent customer demand across the year with no severe seasonal decline.

### Recommended Action
Investigate the marketing campaigns or promotions conducted during the peak months and replicate those strategies during slower months to improve overall revenue.

---

## Insight 2: South Region Generates the Highest Sales

### Observation
The South region records the highest total sales among all regions.

### Data Evidence
Regional Sales chart:
- South: **64.7M**
- North: **54.6M**
- West: **48.9M**
- East: **48.9M**

### Business Interpretation
The South region is the strongest performing market and contributes the largest share of company revenue.

### Recommended Action
Study the business practices, customer preferences, and promotional strategies used in the South region and apply successful approaches to lower-performing regions.

---

## Insight 3: East and West Regions Have Growth Opportunities

### Observation
East and West generate noticeably lower sales than South.

### Data Evidence
Both East and West contribute approximately **49M** in sales, which is around **15–16M lower** than South.

### Business Interpretation
These regions may have lower customer demand, weaker marketing performance, or fewer high-performing products.

### Recommended Action
Increase regional marketing activities, evaluate inventory distribution, and identify products with strong local demand to improve sales performance.

---

## Insight 4: Technology Products Generate Strong Profit

### Observation
Technology products are among the most profitable categories.

### Data Evidence
Sub-categories such as:
- Copiers
- Accessories
- Phones
- Machines

generate profits ranging from approximately **6.4M to 7.3M**.

### Business Interpretation
Technology products provide significantly higher profitability compared to many furniture and office supply products.

### Recommended Action
Increase inventory availability and promotional focus for high-profit technology products while ensuring adequate stock levels.

---

## Insight 5: Higher Discounts Reduce Profitability

### Observation
Profit generally decreases as discount percentage increases.

### Data Evidence
The Discount vs Profit scatter plot shows a downward trend line indicating a negative relationship between discount and profit.

### Business Interpretation
Heavy discounting reduces profit margins even when sales continue.

### Recommended Action
Introduce discount limits and evaluate promotional effectiveness to maximize profitability without excessive price reductions.

---

## Insight 6: Home Office Customers Generate the Highest Sales

### Observation
Among customer segments, Home Office customers contribute the highest sales.

### Data Evidence
Customer Segment Performance:
- Home Office: **74.5M**
- Consumer: **71.9M**
- Corporate: **70.6M**

### Business Interpretation
Although the difference is modest, Home Office customers currently generate the largest revenue contribution.

### Recommended Action
Develop loyalty programs and targeted promotions specifically for Home Office customers while continuing to grow the other customer segments.

---

## Insight 7: Current Return Rate Is Relatively Low

### Observation
Overall product return rate is approximately **4.55%**.

### Data Evidence
The KPI card shows an overall return rate of **4.55%**.

### Business Interpretation
Most customer orders are completed successfully, indicating satisfactory product quality and order fulfillment performance.

### Recommended Action
Continue monitoring return trends by product category and identify products with unusually high return frequencies before they become larger operational issues.

---

## Insight 8: Business Performance Is Strong but Profit Can Be Improved

### Observation
The company generates strong overall sales while maintaining healthy profitability.

### Data Evidence
Dashboard KPIs:
- Total Sales: **217.0M**
- Total Profit: **33.3M**
- Return Rate: **4.55%**

### Business Interpretation
The business is financially healthy, but further profit growth can be achieved by optimizing discount strategies and expanding high-performing regions and product categories.

### Recommended Action
Focus future investments on profitable product categories, strengthen underperforming regions, monitor discount policies, and continue using dashboard insights to support data-driven business decisions.
